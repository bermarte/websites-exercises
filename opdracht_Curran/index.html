J
ames Curran is a British animator and director currently living in New York and Los Angeles.
He has a background in videos games, music videos, commercials and regularly works for clients such as Disney, Nike and Samsung.
He is perhaps best known for his ‘Gifathon’ projects—30 animated GIFs released in 30 days based on his experiences living in New York City, Los Angeles, and Tokyo.
